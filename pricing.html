Extended Packages Available

For longer events, we offer extended rental packages. The 5-hour package is available for $1,250, and the 6-hour package is $1,450. All packages can be customized to fit your specific needs. Additional hours, extra prints, custom backdrops, and special props can be added. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your event requirements.

Price Range: $550 - $1,450+ depending on package and add-ons

Add-On Options

Enhance your photo booth experience with our add-on options. Unlimited prints are available at $60 per hour of rental time. The glam booth add-on provides black and white Kardashian-style images for $75. Waiting time can be added at $50 per hour if you need the booth set up before your event begins or kept after it ends.

Important Information

  • ✓ No travel fees - We do not charge travel fees. However, longer distances may be subject to a three-hour minimum rental time.
  • ✓ Venue accessibility - Venues with multiple flights of stairs and no elevator may be subject to additional fees.
  • ✓ Deposit - A $100 deposit is required to secure your event date.
