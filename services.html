What We Offer

Comprehensive photo booth rental services tailored to your event needs

Professional Equipment

High-quality DSLR cameras and studio lighting ensure crisp, clear photos every time. Our professional-grade equipment delivers results that match what you'd expect from a professional photography studio.

Instant Prints

Guests receive their photos immediately with our professional-grade printers. Each photo is printed on high-quality paper, ready to take home as a keepsake from your event.

Digital Sharing

All photos are available digitally via email or text message, making it easy for guests to share their memories on social media. We provide a seamless digital experience alongside physical prints.

On-Site Attendant

Our experienced attendants ensure everything runs smoothly throughout your event. They help guests, manage the equipment, and make sure everyone has a great experience with the photo booth.

Custom Backdrops

Choose from our collection of luxury backdrops or customize one to match your event theme. Premium and deluxe packages include custom backdrop options to perfectly complement your event's aesthetic.

Props Collection

Fun props and accessories to add personality and humor to every photo. We bring a variety of props including hats, glasses, signs, and themed accessories to make your photos memorable.

Custom Templates

Personalized photo templates with your event details, names, or branding. Add your wedding date, company logo, or custom messaging to make each photo unique to your event.

Online Gallery

Premium and deluxe packages include access to an online gallery where all event photos are stored. Guests can easily access and download all photos from your event in one convenient location.
